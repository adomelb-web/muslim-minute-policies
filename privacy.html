Privacy Policy - The Muslim Minute

Last updated: April 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how The Muslim Minute handles information when using our automated posting service.

1. Information We Collect

This service does not collect personal data from viewers. We store only the OAuth tokens necessary to post content to TikTok and YouTube on behalf of the channel owner.

2. How We Use Information

OAuth tokens are used solely to authenticate and publish Islamic reminder videos to @themuslimminute3 on TikTok and YouTube.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any data with third parties. Tokens are stored locally on the operator device only.

4. Third-Party Platforms

This service posts to TikTok and YouTube. Their privacy policies govern how those platforms handle your data.

5. Security

Access tokens are stored locally and are never transmitted to any server other than the official TikTok and YouTube APIs.

6. Contact

Questions? YouTube: @MuslimMinuteDaily
